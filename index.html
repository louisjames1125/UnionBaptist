About Us

Who is Union Baptist?

Union Baptist Church is a Christ-centered community dedicated to worship, spiritual growth, and serving others. Rooted in faith and guided by Scripture, we strive to share God’s love through meaningful worship, fellowship, and outreach. We welcome individuals and families to grow together in faith, build strong relationships, and live out the teachings of Jesus Christ.

Contact Us

Community:

Union Baptist Church is a welcoming faith community where people come together to worship, grow spiritually, and support one another. We value fellowship, unity, and building strong relationships rooted in Christ.

Mission:

Our mission is to share the Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurture spiritual growth through biblical teaching, and serve our community with love, compassion, and faithfulness.

Inspiration:

We are inspired by God’s Word, the life and teachings of Jesus Christ, and the work of the Holy Spirit. Our faith guides us to worship with purpose, serve with joy, and live out God’s love each day.
