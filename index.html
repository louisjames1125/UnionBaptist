About Us

Who is Union Baptist?

Union Baptist Church is a Christ-centered community dedicated to worship, spiritual growth, and serving others. Rooted in faith and guided by Scripture, we strive to share God’s love through meaningful worship, fellowship, and outreach. We welcome individuals and families to grow together in faith, build strong relationships, and live out the teachings of Jesus Christ.

Contact Us

Community:

Union Baptist Church is a friendly church you can call home. Anyone is welcome to worship, grow spiritually, and support one another.

Mission:

We are a bible believing and teaching church. Our mission is to share the Gospel of Jesus Christ and serve our community with love, compassion, and faithfulness.

Inspiration:

We are inspired by God’s Word, the life and teachings of Jesus Christ, and the work of the Holy Spirit. Our faith guides us to worship with purpose.
